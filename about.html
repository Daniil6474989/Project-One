About Us

Welcome to Karicger — your cozy corner for natural delights, artisanal sweets, and wholesome treats. Founded in 2020, we believe in the power of simple, honest ingredients to bring joy to your table.

Our team of passionate bakers and foragers sources the finest local produce, honey, nuts, and dairy to craft unforgettable flavors. Every product is made with love, care, and a sprinkle of magic.

We are proudly based in the heart of Moscow, Russia — where tradition meets innovation.

Visit our flagship store or order online — we deliver warmth and flavor to your doorstep.
